  • Turning brands into friendly heroes
    Marketing through well-employed social media is a two-way street. Building on a relationship between the brand and the consumers, it spreads awareness and brings products and services to them instantly and intimately. When a digital strategy is well deployed and creates positive buzz, it drives users to engage in a dialogue that builds loyalty through active online communities. By placing Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest at the heart of the media strategy, we support agencies and direct clients to smartly interact with their audience leading them to the marketing objectives behind every one of their campaigns.
  • The master plan
    Our social media services begin with a detailed plan based on client marketing and business objectives. We isolate key platforms that present opportunities for your company and outline ways to develop, manage, and maintain those platforms. Our role is crucial when it comes to defining key platforms; we then develop them according to the brief and work narrowly on managing and sustaining them in time. Our team works hand in hand to implement each social media initiative with complete custom profiles, engaging content backed with ongoing tracking & optimization.
  • Community engagement
    Converting a group of consumers to an online community requires continuous interaction through social media. When engagement is meaningful, consumers become part of the brand and later on turn into active ambassadors. The online "rules of engagement" are simple but critical. We emphasize on the design of an appealing interface that holds one-of-a-kind applications fueled with pertinent content that triggers participation; we work closely on optimizing customized Facebook and YouTube pages, absorbing Twitter campaigns and Pinterest profiles alongside corporate LinkedIn profiles loaded with real brand value.
